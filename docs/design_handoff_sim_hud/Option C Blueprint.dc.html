Reachability floor 40 → 100, plus distance-weighted attendance
The party-emergence ≤40 A* bound was tuned for 96×64; a corner hamlet sits ~95 tiles from the central tavern, so the floor rises to 100 (every door can reach the tavern). It is not the attendance threshold: invite/attend is weighted by travel cost — attendProb = 1 − pathTiles/70 — so nearby hamlets pack the tavern and far ones show up for the big events. That's what makes the world feel real, and it makes the hub genuinely central without forcing anyone.
