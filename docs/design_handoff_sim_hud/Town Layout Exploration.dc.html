What the generator must change — any option
1 · Kill the uniform road grid
Stop laying full-width verticals + horizontals first. Place buildings into packed districts, then thread tight paths only where agents actually walk. The grid is what creates the empty lots.
2 · Raise decor density ~30×, in clusters
Drop the decor.length < 16 cap. Target ~400–540 trees/bushes/flowers as clumps and rim woodland, never one-per-cell. Clusters read as forest; singles read as polka dots.
3 · Bigger, multi-room buildings
Use the modular/Victorian sets you already credited for an aisled store, a school, an apartment block. Footprint coverage ~22%, abutting where it makes sense — not 5×5 boxes floating in green.
4 · Fill interiors (~1,300 pieces)
Your wooden-furniture + interiors packs are sitting unused. Bed, table, chairs, shelves, rugs, plants per room — the single biggest "alive" lever, and it's pure placement.
5 · Vary the ground
Two grass shades + transition tiles + flower tufts (all credited) break the flat-green field. This is your deferred "Phase 2–7 dressing" — it's the cheapest, highest-impact phase, do it first.
6 · Size, only if needed
Option B shows 96×64 works packed. Go 140×100 (the_ville) for breathing room and ~25 agents — but density first, or a bigger map is just more empty grass.
